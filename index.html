  2. Introduction

    Growing up in a small mountain town gave me plenty of opportunities, from scholarships to fun trips and everything between. While I was plenty busy back home, the small town left me with a yearning to try new things and meet new people. That desire has stayed with me in my academic career and personal life, since I have always been willing to take an obscure class or discover something entirely unique. After finding my love for software development because of that curiosity, I decided to move on to another place to learn how to code.


    Now, I am a student at the Colorado School of Mines and am majoring in Computer Science. Why choose a stem major like computer science? Simply put, it is what I love to do. Coding is one of my passions and having the power to create anything with some programming is just one of the many perks.


    Apart from CS, I always enjoy taking a hike around the town of Golden and into the mountains of Colorado. Similarly, I am fond of a good backpacking trip or a possible camping adventure. During the winter, and with what free time I have at school, one could find me sitting down with some friends and playing a board game or two.


    Moving forward, I hope to become a software developer and data scientist. I believe that the future of computer science lies in the analysis and development surrounding big data, so that is where I honed my career and academics. It is anyone's guess as to what company I will end up with, and it may even be my own, but I do know that I will succeed, and with that mindset, I will be able to go anywhere.
